About Us.

Pizza Topia is no ordinary pizza place as it serves not just your taste-buds' desires but your heart's as well. Pizza Topia came to existence due to the passionate nature of it's founder, Marvin Kimathi, who wanted to provide people with pizzas that are not only delicous but satisfying as well. We not only create wonderful pizzas but also amazing bonds and interactions as well.

“It’s not just a meal, it’s a destination,” says Marvin. “People come here to relax and enjoy the setting, not to grab a meal and run.”

"When the sun sets behind the scenic Ngong Hills, the light bathes everything in beautiful magenta tones, you cannot admire the breathtaking spectacle whilst indulging in the restaurant's mouth-watering highlights and fail to feel the strong life force energy. The aliveness, also known as the Marvin Kimathi."

The white-settler community and the tourists loved it, the locals made it popular, fast-forward nine years later and the restaurant's 7 branches bake 500 pizzas in four hours.
